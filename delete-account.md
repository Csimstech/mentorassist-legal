# MentorAssist — Delete Your Account

This page explains how to delete your MentorAssist account and all associated data.

## Developer
MentorAssist by CSimsTech

## How to request account deletion

**Option 1: From inside the app**
1. Open MentorAssist
2. Tap the menu (☰) in the top-left
3. Tap Settings
4. Scroll to the Account section
5. Tap "Delete my account"
6. Confirm

**Option 2: By email**
If you cannot access the app, email us at: mentorassist.support@gmail.com
Include your account email address or user ID. We will delete your account and data within 7 days.

## What data is deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:
- Your user profile (age, profession, interests, goals, category preferences)
- Your weekly challenge assignments and reflections
- Your progress history (streaks, levels, completion data)
- Your notification preferences
- Your subscription records (RevenueCat billing records may be retained for tax purposes)

## What data is retained

We may retain the following for legal and accounting compliance:
- Billing records (if you subscribed) — retained for 7 years as required by tax law
- Anonymized usage statistics (no personal identifiers)
